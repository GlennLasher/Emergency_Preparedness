====== Personal Radio Services ======
Created Monday 26 December 2022

The FCC defines a number of different types of two-way radio services as "Personal Radio Services."  There are four that are specifically focused on voice communications via handheld transceivers (HT's or "walkie-talkies") or mobile radios.

[[+Citizens Band (CB)]]
[[+Family Radio Service (FRS)]]
[[+General Mobile Radio Service (GMRS)]]
[[+Multi Use Radio Service (MURS)]]
